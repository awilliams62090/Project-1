Our Mission


HomewardFound is a web application that helps people to find their lost pets. This app will allow users to log in using their Facebook account and create a profile for their lost pet, which can be shared with the HomewardFound community, and posted to social media. In turn, the user can notify when the community has been found, and distribute a lost-and-found reward using a third-party payment service. Post to the HomewardFound community about their lost pet/animal and share via social media, facebook/instagram/twitter. Our community will be able to post updates to the owner of seen location of the animal, if it has been checked in to a pound/humane society/animal hospital, or contact the owner if the animal has been found. The user can also notify the community when their animal has been found/returned home. Users in the community can use this app to better the world by checking the site for lost pets and become their communities own vigilante superhero by returning pets to their rightful, loving owners.
